About Us


Welcome to Computer Zone, your ultimate destination for top-notch computer goods! At Computer Zone, we are passionate about technology and dedicated to providing our customers with the highest quality products and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2002, Computer Zone was established with a mission to bring the latest technology to our community. Starting as a small business, we aimed to offer a wide range of computer products at competitive prices while ensuring a personalized shopping experience. Over the years, our dedication to these principles has helped us grow into a trusted name in the tech industry.

What We Offer

At Computer Zone, we pride ourselves on our extensive selection of computer goods, including:

  • Desktops & Laptops: From high-performance gaming rigs to sleek and portable ultrabooks, we have something for every need and budget.
  • Components & Peripherals: Whether you're building a custom PC or upgrading your current setup, you'll find everything you need, from processors and motherboards to keyboards and mice.
  • Networking Solutions: Stay connected with our range of routers, modems, and networking accessories designed to keep you online and running smoothly.
  • Software & Accessories: Discover the latest software solutions and essential accessories to enhance your computing experience.
